Case Study: Contract Based Employment

About the Project

The client, a multi-billion dollar telecom provider headquartered in Germany, was in search of a partner who could support their efforts to overhaul their IT systems of their data center through contract based employment.

With little ramp up time, the client required a team of seasoned Build Engineers (Server & Database) that would be able to complete the project, which was slated to be long term assignment from conception to completion. Additionally, some professionals hired for this project would need to work onsite throughout the duration of the project, which proved to be a difficult staffing assignment as most contractors with quality experience typically work remotely on a project-toproject basis

With this in mind, the client sought an agency that they could trust, to outline a cost-effective recruitment strategy that would set the project up for successful delivery. Hence, the Stakefield team was roped in.

The Challenge

Niche + Hybrid IT Skill Sets: The client needed to secure professionals with good knowledge in Data Center environment with enterprise database technologies. In addition to ITIL, SOX and security regulations the professionals should have well experience in server script languages including PL/SQL.

Different Timezone Working Hours: Since the client had to staff for a combination of senior and junior roles with varying levels of experience, securing this highly niche talent who are willing to work on Central European Timezone (CET) would be challenging.

The Outcome

Equipped with a team of recruiters with extensive backgrounds in IT staffing, the Stakefield team needed to provide a quick turnaround in delivering the professionals for each phase of the project. Over the course of few weeks, the team identified, and onboarded all engineers.

The Stakefield team’s knowledge of the IT market as well as the rapport they had built with the Director of IT proved to be critical in their ability to outline a strategy that educated the client on the best possible strategy to employ for a successful project delivery.
